Dr. Anju Mehta is the Lead Medical Aesthetic Doctor at RootsAesthetics Clinic, bringing over 20 years of clinical experience as an NHS Anaesthetist to her aesthetic medicine practice in London.

After completing the prestigious Ofqual-regulated Level 7 Diploma in injectables (JCCP-approved), Dr. Mehta transitioned to full-time aesthetic medicine, where she now leads the team at RootsAesthetics, specializing in natural-looking, rejuvenating treatments.

With a special interest in regenerative medicine, Dr. Mehta's meticulous attention to detail and calm, reassuring demeanor have made her one of London's most sought-after aesthetic doctors. Her approach focuses on long-term patient relationships and personalized treatment plans.

As the medical lead at RootsAesthetics, Dr. Mehta ensures all treatments meet the highest medical standards while delivering natural-looking results that enhance rather than transform.
